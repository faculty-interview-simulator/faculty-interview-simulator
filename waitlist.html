Frequently Asked Questions

Key details about Faculty Interview Client, the desktop academic interview preparation software for faculty candidates.

What is Faculty Interview Client?

Faculty Interview Client is desktop software for academic job candidates preparing for faculty interviews, lecturer interviews, postdoc interviews, and assistant professor interviews.

Who is this software for?

It is designed for PhD students, postdocs, lecturers, research fellows, and assistant professor candidates preparing for academic job market interviews.

What does the full version include?

The full desktop client includes more than 200 faculty interview questions, timed mock interview mode, searchable question libraries, and local progress tracking.

Does it require an online account?

No. The desktop client is designed to run locally. Your practice progress is stored on your own device, not in a hosted database.

Which platforms are supported?

The full desktop client is being prepared for macOS and Windows. The free online practice version can be used directly in the browser.

Is this an AI interview tool?

The first version focuses on structured practice, question coverage, timing, and preparation workflow rather than AI scoring or automated hiring advice.

Is this affiliated with a university?

No. Faculty Interview Client is an independent preparation tool. It is not affiliated with any university, department, hiring committee, or search committee.
